National Hill Veiw Public School Banashankari(BSK)(Unit II), established in 2013 in Bengaluru, is a premier CBSE-affiliated co-educational institution fostering holistic development through a humanistic approach. The school serves students from playgroup to grade 12, focusing on nurturing compassionate, future-ready leaders with strong ethical values.
